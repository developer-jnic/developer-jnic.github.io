The Cybersecurity Research National Conferences (JNIC) are the reference scientific congress acting as the meeting point where different actors working in the field of cybersecurity research (universities, technological and research centres, companies and public authorities) can exchange knowledge and experience with the shared goal of strengthening research in the Cybersecurity field at the national level.

Stay tuned for more info regarding 2022 edition.

JNIC2022 Will be hold in Bilbao - June 2022. It will be the seventh edition and organized by Tecnalia with the usual collaboration of INCIBE.

- JNIC2022

JNIC2021 LIVE held Online - June 2021 and organized by University of Castilla-La Mancha with the usual collaboration of INCIBE.

- JNIC2021 LIVE

JNIC2019 held in Cáceres - June 2019 and organized by its first time for more than one institution, namely University of Extremadura, University Complutense of Madrid and COMPUTAEx, with the collaboration of INCIBE.

- JNIC2020

JNIC2018 held in San Sebastián - June 2018 and organized by Mondragon Unibersitatea. Fourth edition was supported by many sponsors. The scientific congress included a complete activities program, and presented the winning solution proposals to the scientific challenges of the Tranference Programme.

- JNIC2018

JNIC2017 held in Madrid - May and June 2017 organized by URJC. Third edition with a great attendance, introduced the sponsorship support, and presented the first edition of the Tranference Programme

- JNIC2017

JNIC2016 held in Granada - Junio 2016 organized by UGR. Second edition that consolidated the internal governance of the conference, and structures of its committees. It generated the JNIC bylaws and laid the foundation for the selection of next edition venue.

- JNIC2016

JNIC2015 held in León - September 2015 organized by Unileón. First Edition that served as the starting point of this initiative with the collaboration of the research community in cybersecurity.
